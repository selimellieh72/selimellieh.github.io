Project Overview

In the ever-evolving landscape of social media, understanding public sentiment has become crucial. The 'TwitterHashtagScrapper' addresses this need with precision. This powerful Python script, initially tailored for a specific client, enables the scraping of a vast array of tweets linked to any chosen hashtag. Its versatility lies in its configurability and customization options, making it an indispensable tool for data analysts and marketers seeking to gauge public opinion and trends through Twitter.
