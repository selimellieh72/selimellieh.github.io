Project Overview

The project involved creating an online presence for Dr. Gregoire Abi Chaker, a renowned forensic doctor based in Lithuania. The primary objective was to design a website that not only highlighted Dr. Abi Chaker's expertise in forensic medicine but also ensured high visibility through effective search engine optimization (SEO). By prioritizing SEO, the website significantly increased Dr. Abi Chaker's online visibility, attracting a substantial number of clients who discovered his services through internet searches.

Additionally, the website was designed with language localization capabilities, offering content in both English and Lithuanian. This feature was particularly important in catering to Lithuania's diverse population and international clients seeking forensic medical services. The bilingual nature of the site not only broadened its accessibility but also enhanced user experience, reflecting Dr. Abi Chaker's commitment to serving a wide range of clients. Overall, the website successfully positioned Dr. Gregoire Abi Chaker as a key player in the field of forensic medicine in Lithuania, while also reaching an international audience.
